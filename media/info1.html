What is a cryptoparty?


First you must understand the concept of cryptoparties in general,
according to the cryptoparty official website;

"CryptoParty is a decentralized movement with events happening all over the world."

Cryptoparties are descentralized cypherpunk events built by communities with the intention to trade knowledge of technology, cryptography, art and
more. During the event there are many workshops, art expos, and music shows people can attend to.

We are building the first hicetnunc cryptoparty festival experience
based on the web metaverse, so everyone in the world can join us with computers or VR devices.
